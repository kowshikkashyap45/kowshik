Srinivasa Ramanujan

Known for his contributions to mathematical analysis, number theory, infinite series, and continued fractions.

Bhaskara II

Famous for his work in algebra, arithmetic, and calculus. He wrote the book "Siddhanta Shiromani".

Aryabhata

One of the earliest Indian mathematicians and astronomers, known for his work on the approximation of pi and the concept of zero.
